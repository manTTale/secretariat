Welcome to the secretariat of the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration

What is the FEAA secretary application?

You are now logged in as a Secretary. The FEAA secretariat app allows secretaries to easily create custom documents which will be completed by the students and also review them after they are submitted

What are the functionalities of the FEAA secretary application?

This app offers the functionality of creating documents using drag and drop technology in order to make the document creation process as smooth as possible. It also allows the secretaries to review the documents submitted by the students.

Welcome to the secretariat of the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration

What is the FEAA secretary application?

You are now logged in as an Administrator. The FEAA secretariat app allows administrators to manage the students and the secretaries of the whole faculty.

What are the functionalities of the FEAA secretary application?

This app offers the functionality of creating students and secretaries accounts. These accounts could then be used as login information. It also allows administrators to edit information of the already created students and secretaries accounts in case of a mistake. Administrators can also delete accounts of the aforementioned students or secretaries.

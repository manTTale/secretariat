Welcome to the secretariat of the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration

What is the FEAA secretary application?

You are now logged in as a Student. The FEAA secretariat app allows students to complete documents created by the FEAA secretaries. This process will be done fully online so that you won't have to go physically to the FEAA secretariat

What are the functionalities of the FEAA secretary application?

This app offers the functionality of viewing the documents the student needs to complete. This process will be done fully online so that you won't have to go physically to the FEAA secretariat.
